Ingredients

  • 120 g Chicken breast cut into strips
  • 1 tsp Butter
  • 35 g Common mushrooms sliced
  • 40 g Brown onion sliced
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 1/4 cup Water
  • 1 Chicken stock cube
  • 30 g Sour cream
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard Sugar free
  • 60 g Zucchini Spiralized
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a large frying pan, add mushrooms, onions and garlic. Saute' on low heat until onion is brown. Remove from pan and set aside
  2. In the same pan, add chicken strips, browning lightly
  3. Add water, chicken stock, sour cream and mustard to the pan, and continue cooking over a low heat until chicken is cooked through and the sauce has reduced and thickened
  4. Meanwhile, in another pan, add zucchini to a hot pan with salt and oil and cook for 1 - 2 minutes or until slightly browned. Remove from pan and add to serving bowl
  5. Add mushrooms to chicken dish and stir well until heated through. Serve with spiralized zucchini

notes

Tip: Keep half the garlic dressing aside if you are planning on having the second serving the next day.
